# Banex Mall - Premium Cinematic Design Foundation

## Creative Agency Vision

This is not a shopping center website. This is a luxury destination experience that happens to house retail. Every pixel must whisper exclusivity while shouting discovery.

- **Deep Navy Core**: `#0A0E27` - The void, the infinite, the premium canvas
- **Royal Primary**: `#2E3192` - The crown jewel, regal authority

- **Glass White**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.9)` - Ethereal, floating above the depths

## Layer 7 — Responsive Luxury

### Mobile-First Breakpoints
- **Mobile Intimate**: `320px` - `767px` - Personal, handheld experience
- **Tablet Gallery**: `768px` - `1023px` - Intimate viewing, larger gestures
- **Desktop Cinema**: `1024px` - `1439px` - The full reveal, expansive canvas
- **Ultra-Wide Monument**: `1440px`+ - The architectural scale

### Adaptive Typography
- **Hero Monument**: `4rem` → `8rem` across breakpoints
- **Section Headers**: `2rem` → `4rem` with viewport scaling
- **Body Editorial**: `1rem` → `1.125rem` for optimal readability
- **Micro Copy**: `0.875rem` → `1rem` for accessibility compliance

### Responsive Interactions
- **Touch Targets**: `44px` minimum, `56px` optimal for thumb navigation
- **Hover Alternatives**: Focus states and active feedback for touch devices
- **Gesture Support**: Horizontal scrolling for card collections with visible scroll indicators

## Layer 8 — Accessibility in Luxury

### Color Contrast Standards
- **Primary Text**: `7:1` ratio against dark backgrounds
- **Accent Elements**: `4.5:1` minimum for orange interactions
- **Glass Surfaces**: `3:1` minimum with border reinforcement
- **Focus Indicators**: `2px` solid outline with `4px` offset

### Motion Sensitivity
- **Respects prefers-reduced-motion**: Disables parallax and complex transitions
- **Alternative Animations**: Fade and opacity changes only when motion is disabled
- **Duration Limits**: Maximum `0.5s` for any single animation sequence

### Screen Reader Luxury
- **Semantic Hierarchy**: Proper heading structure for premium content flow
- **Descriptive Labels**: Action-oriented CTA text, not generic "click here"
- **Live Regions**: Status updates for dynamic content like search results

## Layer 9 — Content Strategy as Curation

### Editorial Voice
- **Headlines**: Command attention without shouting - "Discover Banex Mall" not "Welcome to Our Mall"
- **Descriptions**: Create FOMO through exclusivity - "Where Lagos comes to discover what's next"
- **CTAs**: Action with prestige - "Explore Collections" not "View Stores"

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Hero Statement**: One powerful sentence that captures the entire experience
- **Supporting Narrative**: Maximum two lines of context, then immediate action
- **Social Proof**: Curated selection, not overwhelming choice - "Featured this week" not "All 200+ stores"

### Discovery Flow
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Reveal complexity through exploration, not overwhelming initial presentation
- **Curated Paths**: Guide visitors through intentional journeys, not endless browsing
- **Moment of Delight**: Surprise and reward exploration with unexpected discoveries
