# Banex Mall UI Inspiration Brief

## Reference sites
- Lakeside Shopping Centre https://lakeside-shopping.com/
- Bluewater https://www.bluewater.co.uk/en 
- Westfield London https://www.westfield.com/en/united-kingdom/london

## What these sites do well
- Retail-first navigation
- Category-led browsing
- Strong plan-your-visit utility
- Promotions and live offers
- Brand/store discovery cards
- Wayfinding and centre map emphasis

## Page structure pattern
1. Sticky nav
2. Hero section
3. Quick category actions
4. Featured brands/stores
5. Offers / events
6. Plan your visit / map
7. Utility footer

## Reusable UI patterns
- Large hero banner
- Rounded cards
- Offer badges
- Category chips
- Store cards
- Map teaser
- Sticky CTA
- Utility panels

## Visual language
- Premium retail
- Spacious layouts
- Dark or deep brand surfaces
- Strong accent color usage
- High-contrast typography
- Clean editorial grid
- Soft shadows and rounded corners

## Interaction language
- Hover lift on cards
- Smooth fade-up sections
- Horizontal scrolling chips on mobile
- Sticky nav on scroll
- Badge states for live / new / ending soon
- CTA emphasis above the fold

## Content tone
- Confident
- Helpful
- Inviting
- Discovery-led
- Visitor-first

## Do
- Make the mall feel active today
- Make store browsing feel curated
- Make visit planning easy
- Make promos feel alive

## Do not
- Do not make it look like a generic business site
- Do not use dense text blocks
- Do not use flat boring cards
- Do not hide key visitor actions
